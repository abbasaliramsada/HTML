How can we help you ?


If you ordering any item

  1. Go to your home page in our site
  2. Click buy
  3. Click Placeorder
  4. Enter a domain name and extension in the search field, then click Search.
  5. If the domain is available, click Get It to register it directly on Wix. Tip: If the domain you enter is not available, go back to step 2 and try another domain name and/or extension.
  6. Select the registration period and click Continue.
  7. Select a Privacy Protection level.
  8. Click Continue.
